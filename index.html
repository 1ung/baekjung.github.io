About Us

백정Baekjung, a quintessential real ale pub in one of the most historic streets in Singapore.

We have nine real ale pumps and a constantly evolving beer list served from the architecturally wonderful backstreets of Waterloo, overall making 백정Baekjung a unique experience.

Our multi-winning “pub of the year” site is about as traditional as traditional pubs get, but with a Thai twist coming out of its popular kitchen too.

We are open from 11am-11pm Monday to Saturday and 12pm-10:30pm on Sundays (child-friendly until 7pm).

Food & Drink

With beers from Dark Star, Ringwood, Adnams and Brains to name a few, we source new and inspired brews locally and far afield. If you’re visiting for lunch or dinner, Kanchana’s Kitchen provide our fine freshly cooked Thai food 7 days a week.
